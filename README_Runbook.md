## Licensing Requirements Covered

- Base license required: M365 E3, E5, Business Basic/Standard/Premium, or Office 365 E1/E3/E5
- Copilot add-on: $30/user/month (Enterprise, annual); $18–21/user/month (Business)
- Monthly billing available at +5% premium (since December 2024)
- App update channel requirement: Current Channel or Monthly Enterprise Channel only
